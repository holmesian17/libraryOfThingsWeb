Library of Things - About
 

Collection Development Policy

Overview

The Library of Things will exist to serve the people of Framingham by providing a collection of materials that may be prohibitively expensive, new, unusual, or rarely used. The purpose of this collection is to give the community the opportunity to work with a wide variety of objects for learning, practical use, and engagement.

Policy

The Library of Things working group will make purchases based on a number of factors:

  1. The needs of the community
    We should be responsive to the changing needs of our community. Our acquisitions will be made based on professional judgment and input from our patrons. These purchases will be made under the considerations of budget, community need, and projected use.

  2. New and emerging technologies
    Technology is ever changing and new technology is released regularly. Because of this, it can be costly and difficult to keep up to date with the latest devices. Framingham wants to make these materials available for our patrons to take home and play with before deciding whether it is something that they would be interested in purchasing. We will attempt to stay current with with our collection.
  3. Difficult or impractical items for a person to own
    Some items are prohibitively expensive or rarely used to the degree that it would be impractical for someone to invest in them. Offering these kinds of materials would allow our community to do what they need to without having to make a costly purchase.

The Library of Things does accept donations that are in great condition, meet the needs of our collection, and are outright gifts. These will be accepted at the library’s discretion. We also allow patrons to suggest items for the library to consider purchasing.

Our collection will not circulate among libraries other than the Framingham Main Library and the McAuliffe Branch Library. The length of time for borrowing each item is variable depending on the item and will be reflected in your library account and included in the materials alongside the item.
